# Query Faithfulness Relevancy Precision Recall Pass

💡 Why These Evaluation Metrics?

  • Faithfulness — Critical for commerce: agents must not hallucinate prices, specs, or stock. A hallucinated "$99 laptop" leads to trust erosion and cart abandonment.
  • Answer Relevancy — Measures if the agent addresses the user's actual shopping intent rather than going off-topic.
  • Context Precision — With k=5 retrieval, we track how many of those 5 results are actually useful. This justifies our k choice.
  • Context Recall — Ensures our retrieval doesn't miss relevant products. Low recall means users don't see options they'd want.
  • Conversion Rate — Ultimate business KPI: what % of conversations lead to a purchase?
  • Average Comparison Depth — Users who compare ≥2 products are 3x more likely to purchase. This tracks engagement quality.

Why k=5? Research in consumer psychology (Iyengar & Lepper, 2000) shows that 5-6 options optimize the balance between choice satisfaction and decision paralysis. More options decrease purchase likelihood; fewer options feel restrictive.
